For Part Two we will ask you to make a decision as a Seller, with a separate decision for each of the three types of urn. However, in each of the three decisions, you will be matched with a Robot Buyer. The Robot Buyer will draw an ideal quantity X from the relevant urn, but has been programmed to purchase the number of widgets that gives the Robot Buyer the highest possible payoff.

If you are selected for Payment in Part Two, one of your three decisions will be randomly selected. We will fairly draw an ideal quantity X for the Robot Buyer from the given urn, and the Robot Buyer will then make a purchase choice from the widget options you selected that maximizes the Robot Buyer's payoff. If selected for payment in Part Two, your payoff will be $8 plus $4 for every widget purchased by the Robot Buyer.

Taking on the role of the Seller, please make a decision for each type of urn.
