In Parts Three to Five we restore the Buyer's option to walk away and purchase zero widgets. However, in all of the subsequent parts of this session you will take on the role of the Seller. Instead of interacting with human Buyer participants in this room, you will be matched with a large number of Robot Buyers trained in previous sessions to act like human participants.

By matching you to many of these robots and taking averages across their actions, we can eliminate randomness and offer you fixed payments for any decision you might make as the Seller.

Your role in Parts Three to Five is to use your experiences from Part One to make decisions that you think will maximize this fixed payment.
