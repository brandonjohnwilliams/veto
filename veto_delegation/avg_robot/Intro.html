{{ block title }} Parts Three to Five Overview {{ endblock }} {{ block content }}

  • In Parts Three to Five we restore the Buyer's option to walk away and purchase zero widgets just as in Part One.
  • However, in all of the subsequent parts of this session you will take on the role of the Seller.
  • Instead of interacting with human Buyer participants in this room, you will be matched with a large number of Robot Buyers
    • The Robot Buyers have been trained based on the choices of participants in previous sessions.
    • By taking averages across many Robot Buyers, we eliminate randomness in the outcomes
    • Any decision you might make as the Seller will therefore result in a fixed amount: the average payoff for that decision.

Your role in Parts Three to Five is to use your experience from Part One to make decisions that you think will maximize the average payment.
