Part Two will be identical to Part One except that Buyers no longer have the option to walk away.

To begin Part Two you will start as the Seller, choosing {{ if single == 1 }}the number of widgets {{else }}a menu of options (a minimum and maximum number of widgets){{ endif }} to offer to a Buyer participant in the room, just as you did in Part One. However, this Buyer no longer has the option to walk away and must purchase {{ if single == 1 }}the amount of widgets offered to them.{{ else }}an amount of widgets between the minimum and maximum offered to them.{{ endif }} While your payment as Seller will only depend on the amount of widgets {{ if single == 1 }}offered{{ else }}chosen from the menu{{ endif }}, the matched Buyer participant's payment will depend on their ideal number of widgets drawn from the urn just as before.

You will make three choices as the Seller:

  • {{ if single == 1 }}An amount of widgets to offer{{ else }}A menu of widgets to offer{{ endif }} to the Buyer for each of the three types of urn (Low, Middle, and High).
  • For each of your three Seller choices, you will be matched to a different Buyer participant in the room.

To end Part Two you will be the Buyer. {{ if single == 1 }}As you do not have an option to walk away as the Buyer in Part Two, your only role will be to view your drawn ideal quantity and the number of Widgets the matched Seller offered you. {{ else }} As you do not have an option to walk away as the Buyer in Part Two, your only role will be to view your drawn ideal quantity and to make a choice of Widgets from the menu the matched Seller offered you.{{ endif }} You will view three Buyer {{ if single == 1 }}outcomes{{ else }}menus{{ endif }}:

  • One for each of the three types of urn (Low, Middle, and High).
  • Each Buyer {{ if single == 1 }}outcome{{ else }}menu{{ endif }} you view are from three different Seller participants in the room.
  • Note that the three participants for whom you act as Buyer are distinct from the three participants that are matched to your Seller choices.

The computer will randomly select one of the three urns and one participant in the room as the Seller for payment in Part Two; whichever other participant was matched as the Buyer for that Seller/urn will then also be selected for payment.
