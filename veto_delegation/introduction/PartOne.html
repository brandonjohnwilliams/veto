Part one will consist of 15 rounds. In each round, you will either be a Buyer or a Seller

  • If you are the Seller, your job is to choose the {{ if C.single == 0 }} minimum and maximum number of widgets {{else}} the number of widgets {{endif}} you want to make available for purchase to the Buyer.
  • If you are a Buyer, your job is to make a decision on the number of Widgets you would like to purchase from the Seller.
As a Seller your payoffs will increase with the number of widgets purchased. As a Buyer, you will privately be informed on the ideal number of widgets you would like to buy; where your payoff will be larger the closer you are to your ideal amount. {{ endblock }}
