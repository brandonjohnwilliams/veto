The payoffs in a round for both the Buyer and Seller are directly related to the number of widgets purchased:

  • For Buyers the round payoff depends on how close the purchased quantity is to their ideal value X, drawn at random from an urn. Buyersâ€™ maximum payoffs are $30 if they exactly purchase their ideal number of widgets. The payoff the Buyer earns decreases the further the amount they choose to purchase is from their ideal value X.
  • Sellers round payoffs are larger the more widgets are purchased, where they make $8 plus $4 per widget purchased.
